ABOUT US

As mentioned above, our names are Hristiyan Bratov and Lyubomir Georgiev. We are two high school boys who dived into the sphere of web and game development a few years ago. Our passionate about problem solving and implement fun ideas inspired us to continue even deeper into programming. This board game of ours was made to let people get along with the world of the English literature of which we haven’t been much familiar yet. We hope it will be useful for you as well as your knowledge and will make you feel comfortable playing it.
